### O'Raifeartaigh analytic proof
At gv/m = √3, fermion mass eigenvalues are (0, 2-√3, 2+√3) × m.
- Σm = 4m
- Σ√m = √6 × √m
- Q = (Σ√m)²/(Σm) = 6m/(4m × 3) = ... wait
Actually: Q = Σm/(Σ√m)² = 4m / (6m) = 2/3 exactly. ✓

### Miyazawa vs sBootstrap supercharge
- Miyazawa: Q_Miy ~ q† ε_abc q_b q_c (meson → baryon)
- sBootstrap: Q_sB ~ q† L (meson → lepton via quark annihilation)
- Only diagonal mesons M^i_i produce leptons

### Multi-generation superpotential
W = Σ_k m_k Φ_k + X Σ_k Φ_k
→ m²_scalar,k = m²_lepton,k + f²

### Goldstino identification
- Charged pseudo-goldstino: muon (in pion row)
- Neutral goldstino: neutrino ν₁ (paired with π⁰/K⁰)
- Gravitino mass from f = f_π: ~3.5 × 10⁻¹² eV (consistent with global SUSY limit)
